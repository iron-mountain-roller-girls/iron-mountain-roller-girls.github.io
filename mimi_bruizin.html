When did you join and why did you start playing derby?
I joined IMRG in July 2011 as a referee and transitioned this spring to bout skating on the team. My purpose for joining was to push myself to try something new—little did I know that I would totally fall in love with it!

What keeps you going?
My husband and daughters are my biggest fans, and seeing them in the crowd or helping out with our team gives me the biggest boost. Also, my teammates keep me going—my ladies are always there for me, encouraging me to be the best person I can on and off the track.

What's your favorite thing about your team?
It is hard to name just one-there are so many things that make IMRG a great team! We are really funny and can make any situation or statement hilarious!

Where do you hope to see your team in 2 years?
-An official member of WFTDA
- Known in the derby world for our spirit, sportsmanship and love of the game!

What's your favorite thing to wear to practice?
Tank top, shorts, crash pads, and my Animal tall socks (I love the Muppets!)
