Maiden of the Month

October 2014 / Off D-Wal #2

What is your roller derby playing position of choice?
Definitely blocker

What music gets you pumped up?
I like anything with a good beat

Derby injuries?
Last year, I broke my left ankle when I got knocked out of bounds while jamming. But it took three of them to do it. :)

What do you for your non-derby living?
I work at Moon Glo Roller Rink. Sometimes I help at the snack bar and ticket window but usually you can find me running the music and games.

Any derby heroes? Or skaters that inspire you?
Tannibal Lector from Oly is a smooth skater when she is a jammer and a fierce hitter when she is a blocker.

5 fun non-derby facts about yourself?

  1. I am named after my grandfathers: Daniel and Donald.
  2. My mom was a rink rat and put me on skates as soon as I could walk.
  3. I started playing roller hockey when I was 12. I have been to 16 states where I have won regional and national championships. I have also travelled to 7 different countries as a member of the U.S. National team.
  4. I love to cook and often give food gifts at Christmas.
  5. I am a Pittsburgh fan. I love the Penguins, Steelers, and Pirates.

In the time that you’ve been skating, what has been your favorite derby moment/memory?
Recently, I was named MVP blocker. It was a nice surprise since I didn't feel like I did anything special to deserve it.

If you could tell people 1 thing about roller derby what would it be?
What's not to love about a full contact women's sport?

What is your guilty pleasure?
Hair band music

How has roller derby changed your life?
I have made such good friends. Finally for fun…

Pirates or ninjas?
Pirates

Vampires or zombies?
Vampires-like Kate Beckinsale not Robert Pattinson

Unicorns or Fairies?
Fairies
