Maiden of the Month

February 2015 / Roxy Torment #21

What is your roller derby playing position of choice?
All positions... Blocker, pivot, and jammer

What music gets you pumped up?
Childish Gambino

Derby injuries?
Broken tailbone, a concussion, and pulled ligaments in both knees

What do you for your non-derby living?
Be a Mermaid... And a psychology student at Frostburg State University

Any derby heroes? Or skaters that inspire you?
My entire team!

In the time that you have been skating, what has been your favorite derby moment/memory?
Every home bout and of corse the after parties!

What is your guilty pleasure?
Pinteresting, mermaid things and reality TV

If you could tell people 1 thing about roller derby what would it be?
It is worth it.

How has roller derby changed your life?
Roller derby has given me more confidence and it has given me the best family that I could ever ask for. I would do anything for my derby family and I know that they would do anything for me.

In your opinion what is the biggest misconception about roller derby?
That we are not athletes

Pirates or ninjas?
Mermaids!

Vampires or zombies?
Mermaids!

Unicorns or Fairies?
Mermaids!
